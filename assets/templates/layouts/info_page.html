What this site is and why it exists

I've been interested in seeing whether allowing people to give virtual gifts over physical ones could catch on, effectively replacing material products with a cost-free alternative that wouldn't consume any resources to produce or end up in landfill. Christmas seemed like an ideal opportunity to test this out, and the idea eventually evolved into what you see here!
